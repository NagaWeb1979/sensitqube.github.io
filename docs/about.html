Our Mission




The product is targeted to children on the spectrum. We aim to help children with autism to engage with the toy to maintain concentration and remain intrigued. It is made in consideration of their needs and special requirements, (e.g., noise-less fabric ). The toy not only helps children have fun but also learn from it through its interactive and modifiable layout. We offer a wide range of themes that the patches can differ between, and are working to expand it further as well.
